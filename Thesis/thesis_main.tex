    \subsection{ What is DevOps}
    Agile software development is a practis that was populariyed by the \wordhighlight{'Manifesto for Agile Software Development'}, written by various authors~\cite{manifesto}. It priorizizes the rapid adoption to changes and continious evoulution of software over a predifined plan and contract negoation.
    The four core prinzibles are:
    \begin{itemize}[label=\(\star\)]
        \item \textbf{Individuals and interactions} over processes and tools
        \item \textbf{Working software} over comprehensive documentation
        \item \textbf{Customer collaboration} over contract negotiation
        \item \textbf{Responding to change} over following a plan
    \end{itemize}
    The entire manifest is the foundation of several software development methologies and framworks supporting this stregies, such as \wordhighlight{SCRUM} and \wordhighlight{\ac{XP}.}
